Dormify is an online website which aims to offer affordable furniture to people with small spaces while still helping them make their spaces aesthetically pleasing. For this 4-week project we were tasked with intervening in a problem area by constantly reframing of a problem statement. From this resulted a solution that we believed ultimately meets the need of the users.

RESEARCH AND FRAMING

To begin the process, we started by narrowing down our target audience to college students. This is was done to work within a small scope of the many audience dormify targeted. Plus, they were more easily accessible for our upcoming research.

Moving into college can be exciting yet nervous experience especially for students moving far away from home. In an informal conversation had by myself and a student, they expressed how important it is for their dorm rooms to feel like home, especially after a long day of class.


“I’ve never really been the one to care about the look of my room, but ever since moving into college that became an important aspect given I spend all my time there when I’m not in class.

- Student at SFU University


To further inform this, we conducted in-person interviews and a survey with 70 students. Our criteria for this was that they either have to be living in dorms or once did.

INSIGHT

From our research we discovered 3 problems that we found interesting

Interviewees stated that college life is an important experience for them and that their dorm room is part of that experience.

1.

People do want to have a particular look and feel to their rooms but are unsure what exactly it is they want or how to achieve a feel and look to their dorms

2.

We also noted that there was a huge comparison between their rooms at home and the dorm room sizes with ultimately creates a problem of knowing how to properly integrate their personalities into the constraints of a dormitory.

FRAMING THE PROBLEM

We particularly found insight 2 more insightful and saw an opportunity to help students make their dorm rooms truly reflect who they are, and turn this regular space into their comfort space. So, we began to ask ourselves

How might we help college students in making better decisions about their dorm rooms?

SOLUTION

CURATED FEED

Pinterest and Youtube are some of the popular places people visit when looking for inspiration. Learning from Pinterest, we created a curated feed. The feed exist within the website consisting of different media uploaded by dormify customers. It showcases how they styles their dormify products and what they paired with it to give other inspiration.
